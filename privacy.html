Privacy Policy

Effective:

Information we collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the site:

  • Server logs: IP address, user agent, timestamps. Retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, privacy-respecting page view metrics via a first-party analytics provider (e.g. Plausible or Cloudflare Web Analytics). No cross-site tracking or fingerprinting.
  • Newsletter email: If you subscribe, we store the email with our email service provider for the sole purpose of sending the newsletter. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.

Third-party services

We use third parties that may set cookies or collect data per their own policies:

  • Ad networks (e.g. Google AdSense, Ezoic) may use cookies to serve personalized ads. See Google's ad policy.
  • Affiliate networks (Amazon Associates, Impact, ShareASale, etc.) may set tracking cookies when you click an affiliate link.
  • Merchandise is fulfilled by Printful. When you click to purchase, your data is handled per Printful's privacy policy.
  • Embedded third-party content (e.g. RSS article previews) may load from the publisher's domain.

Cookies

Essential cookies are used only for core functionality. Optional analytics and advertising cookies are used only after you consent where required by law (GDPR, UK GDPR, ePrivacy, CPRA).

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal data. To exercise any right, email privacy@grimnir.example.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards but no system is perfectly secure. You acknowledge that any information transmitted over the internet carries inherent risk.
